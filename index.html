Dr. Hongxing Ye joined the Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Cleveland State University in 2016. He received a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago in 2016. He received his B.S. degree in Dept. of Information and Communication Engineering, and M.S. degree in Dept. of Automation from Xi’an Jiaotong University in China. He was a visiting researcher at Energy Division in Argonne National Laboratory from Sept 2015 to Dec 2015.

Dr. Hongxing Ye's research interests include power and energy system, electricity market, renewable integration, large-scale optimization, and cyber-physical system security. His research is supported by National Science Foundation. He received Sigma Xi Research Excellence Award at Illinois Institute of Technology in 2016. He was honored as Outstanding Reviewer for IEEE Transactions on Power Systems and IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy in 2015.
